Wish Merchandising Banners

The front and center of the Wish homepage.

Product

Wish is an online marketplace. My work revolved around homepage banners: promotional banners that live on the top of the Wish homepage; the first thing a user sees when they open the app.

My role

I worked as an Associate Product Manager intern at Wish on the merchandising team. I worked closely with the merchandising lead to lead two major initiatives for the 1st quarter in 2022. My main role was to lead a redesign of the merchandising pages as well as a solution to monitor merchandising banner performance.

Metrics

Homepage banner performance was not possible to measure, despite it being the most prime real estate on the app. The logging and dashboard solution I worked on included two main performance metrics: banner CTR and attributed GMV.
