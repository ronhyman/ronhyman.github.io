My role

I had the privilege of being in a leadership role at the Canadian Undergraduate Technology Conference (CUTC) in my first two years of university. I led the sponsorship team in 2019-2020 and lead the entire team of 30+ students as co-chair in 2020-2021.Â 

About

The conference amassed a total of 4000+ students from all over Canada and even the world 2020 and 2021. The concept is to bring like-minded students interested in STEM together to learn from industry leaders, expand their professional network, and immerse themselves in technology. In 2021, the conference featured over 60 speakers including the President of Shopify, VP at Facebook, Head of Growth at Notion, and more.
